### Video 17 Space Mods - GML - Effects (2/2) - GameMaker Studio 2

#### `obj_particles:`
- for the three particle sprites set middle center for the sprite origin,
- create `obj_particles` and make this object persistent,
- add `obj_particles` to `rm_start`,
- add `obj_particles > create` event - this is where we create all our particles,
> [Particles](https://manual.yoyogames.com/index.htm#t=GameMaker_Language%2FGML_Reference%2FDrawing%2FParticles%2FParticles.htm) are graphic resources that 
are teriffic for drawing fast yet versatile graphics effects in our games. They do not interact with anything they are purly cosmetic.
A [particle system](https://manual.yoyogames.com/index.htm#t=GameMaker_Language%2FGML_Reference%2FDrawing%2FParticles%2FParticle_Systems%2FParticle_Systems.htm) is like a container for all the particle types.

 
- add `obj_particle > clean up` event to destroy the particle system.

- add `obj_particle > room end` event to clear the particle system.
